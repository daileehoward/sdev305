I started taking all kinds of courses at Green River to help me finally decide what I actually wanted to be. I ended up concentrating in accounting, and although I was good with numbers, they didn't leave me coming back for more...

To my amazement, I found something that I was not only good at, but something I actually enjoyed working with. Ever since then, I was accepted into the bachelor's program in Cohort 12 and am getting some real-world experience with coding. On top of that, I'm thankful for being able work as an IT Student Help Desk Employee for the college.
